SLAP Art Collection

Artist Statement

SLAP is an ongoing series of hand-made collages built around a single recurring element: the Magical Flag of Peace. Each piece is assembled by hand — stickers placed deliberately on paper, cardstock, or other substrates — and no two are identical.

The project began as an exploration of repetition and variation: what happens when the same image is placed in slightly different configurations, on different surfaces, with different numbers of copies? What emerges from constraint? The flag is fixed. Everything else is open.

Over time the collection has grown into something closer to a body of research — a catalog of combinations, a meditation on symmetry and chance, and a record of moments when a piece was given away and went on to live somewhere else.

Rarity Index

Each SLAP is assigned a Rarity Index — a score reflecting how statistically unusual its combination of design attributes is across the entire collection.

The index is calculated from the Design attributes: number of stickers, symmetry, pattern, pattern orientation, flag orientation, spin, shape, and flag version. For each attribute, the frequency of that value across all SLAPs is calculated. The rarity of a given piece is derived from the product of these individual frequencies — rarer combinations of attributes produce a higher score.
